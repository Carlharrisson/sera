## Ideal Customer Profiles

Sera works best for teams experiencing operational pain points. These are not theoretical personas. They are real roles, problems, and behaviors that align with how we build.

---

### 1. SaaS Founder

**Stage**

Seed to Series B. Small team. Moving fast. Wearing too many hats.

**Pain**

- Overwhelmed by DMs, forms, and repetitive questions
- Manual operations cobbled together in Sheets and Slack
- No time to hire. Needs working systems immediately

**What they want**

- Automation that works immediately
- One person to own and solve the problem
- No meetings. No strategy docs. Just results

**What they say**

- "I am just doing it all manually right now"
- "We do not have bandwidth to even scope it"
- "Can you just fix this and show me when it is live"

**How we close**

Show them we can launch in days and eliminate a painful workflow without overhead

---

### 2. Head of Ops or RevOps

**Stage**

Post product market fit. Teams getting messy.

**Pain**

- Internal workflows lack efficiency
- Too many disconnected tools
- Time wasted on CRM updates and handoff coordination

**What they want**

- A faster way to deploy internal systems
- Operational visibility without building from scratch
- Streamlined processes without adding more SaaS tools

**What they say**

- "We are duct taping Airtable and Slack together"
- "Sales always forgets to update the CRM"
- "I just want this to run without chasing people"

**How we close**

Speak to hours saved per week, faster sales cycles, and smoother handoffs

---

### 3. Product or Ops-Heavy Team Lead

**Stage**

Operational complexity scaling up. No internal tools team.

**Pain**

- Swamped with manual tasks and alerts
- Team wastes hours gathering info and answering repeated questions
- No time to build internal systems

**What they want**

- Custom feeling internal tools and bots
- Fewer Slack messages, cleaner dashboards
- Automation that integrates with existing workflows

**What they say**

- "We do not need a full tool, just something that works"
- "Right now it is all in someone's head"
- "Can you build a quick view for the team"

**How we close**

Show them we do not overengineer. We scope tight, ship fast, and fit into their stack

---

### 4. Executive Buyer (COO, VP, Head of Ops)

**Stage**

Growth mode. Focused on scaling without bloating the team.

**Pain**

- Hiring is slow. Execution is slower
- Cannot justify full time roles for internal tools
- Needs to standardize operations without complete overhaul

**What they want**

- An external partner who builds like a team member
- Measurable savings in hours, hires, or cost
- Systems that work in practice, not theory

**What they say**

- "We cannot keep hiring just to patch internal gaps"
- "How fast can this go live"
- "How do I know it will actually get used"

**How we close**

Lead with ROI, adoption, and team feedback. Position Sera as execution, not consulting

---

## Common Signals They Are a Fit

- Operations centered in Slack, Airtable, Sheets, Notion
- Frustrated by manual work, handoffs, or update tracking
- Seeks quick results, not lengthy processes
- Prefers async communication
- Has an internal system owner

---

## Who Is Not a Fit

- Wants to explore ideas without clear needs
- Lacks internal champion to own or adopt our build
- Expects decks, audits, or strategy pitches
- Disappears after kickoff
- Prioritizes perfection over shipping
