## Writing philosophy

Sera's writing should reflect how we work. No excess. No delay. No drift.

If a sentence does not help someone move faster it does not belong.

## Core attributes

- Clear not clever
- Concrete not conceptual
- Operational not aspirational
- Outcome driven not opinion driven

## Language to use

- Built in days
- Scoped for speed
- Shipped fast. Adopted immediately
- Saves 20 hours per week
- Used every day
- Replaces manual work
- One system. One owner. Done

## Language to avoid

- Unlock potential
- Empower your team
- Productivity gains
- Digital transformation
- End to end
- Strategic alignment
- Frictionless collaboration

## Formatting guidelines

- Lead every section with pain proof or result
- Use one idea per paragraph
- Break complex points into bullets
- Prefer numbers over adjectives
- Never talk about what something could do
- Only say what it already does or is doing now
